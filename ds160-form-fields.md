# DS-160 Form Fields for Google Form

## Section 1: Personal Information
- Full name (as on passport)
- Full name in native alphabet (Cyrillic)
- Any other names used (maiden name, etc.)
- Date of birth
- City of birth
- Country of birth
- Nationality
- Do you hold any other nationalities? (Y/N + which)

## Section 2: Passport Information
- Passport number
- Passport book number (if any)
- Country/authority that issued passport
- City where issued
- State/province where issued
- Country where issued
- Issuance date
- Expiration date

## Section 3: Contact Information
- Home address (street, city, postal code, country)
- Phone number (primary)
- Phone number (secondary, optional)
- Email address
- Do you have a German residence permit? (Y/N)
- If yes: permit type, expiration date

## Section 4: Travel Information
- Purpose of trip (Tourism / Visiting friends)
- Specific travel plans? (Y/N)
- Intended arrival date (approximate)
- Intended length of stay
- Address where you'll stay in the US (Nikita's address)
- Who is paying for the trip? (Self / Someone else — name, relationship)

## Section 5: US Contact (Nikita)
- Contact name in US
- Organization (if any)
- Relationship to applicant
- US address
- US phone number
- US email

## Section 6: Family Information
- Father's full name
- Father's date of birth
- Is father in the US? (Y/N)
- Mother's full name  
- Mother's date of birth
- Is mother in the US? (Y/N)
- Do you have immediate relatives in the US? (Y/N)
- If yes: name, relationship, status

## Section 7: Work/Education (Current)
- Current occupation
- Employer/School name
- Employer/School address
- Employer/School phone
- Start date of current employment/studies
- Monthly income (optional but helpful)
- Briefly describe duties

## Section 8: Previous Work/Education (last 5 years if different)
- Previous employer/school
- Address
- Job title
- Start/end dates

## Section 9: Education
- Highest level of education completed
- Name of institution
- City/Country
- Course of study
- Dates attended

## Section 10: Previous US Travel
- Have you ever been to the US? (Y/N)
- If yes: dates, length of stay
- Have you ever been issued a US visa? (Y/N)
- If yes: date, type
- Have you ever been refused a US visa? (Y/N)
- If yes: when, where, type

## Section 11: Security Questions (all Y/N)
- Do you have a communicable disease?
- Do you have a mental or physical disorder?
- Are you a drug abuser/addict?
- Have you ever been arrested or convicted?
- Have you ever violated immigration law?
- Have you ever been deported from the US?
- Have you ever worked in the US without authorization?
- Are you seeking to work illegally in the US?
- Have you ever been involved in espionage, terrorism, genocide, torture?
- Were you involved in Nazi persecution?
- Have you ever sought asylum elsewhere?

(Note: Almost all answers should be NO — if any are YES, we need to discuss)

## Section 12: Additional
- Do you have any specialized skills, training, or experience related to firearms, explosives, nuclear, biological, or chemical matters?
- Have you ever served in the military? (Y/N)
- If yes: country, branch, rank, specialty, dates

## Photo
- Recent photo (white background, no glasses, face clearly visible)
